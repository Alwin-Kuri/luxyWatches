References and Comparisons

Reference Homepage

Our Homepage

The homepage of Titan Nepal was really simple and elegant at the same time, so we wanted similar styling with the addition of a well performing slider which automatically changes images in certain duration of time.

Reference Collection

Our Collections

The collection page of Brietling was really well designed with proper functioning wishlist and sliding images of the watch show collection, so we designed it in a rather novice way with taking it as a reference.

Reference Features

Our features

We also noticed the features of Titan Watches, which would have made us realize that the watch store was actually providing a few noticeable features if we were customers. So, we also added a few features with this site as a reference.

Reference Footer

Our Footer

In the brietling page, we noticed a well structured footer with external links redirecting to other websites, so we took it as a reference to implement a similar type of footer but a different design.

Reference Payment

Our Payment

When we clicked the "Buy now" button in Titan Watches, we noticed a very simple yet working payment form. Hence, we styled our payment form in a similar manner with our own skills and a few youtube videos.

Reference Team

Our Team

While surfing through the net, we also found a site with a very simple but briefly descripted "Meet Our Team" page. So, we made a similar design for our "About Us" Page.

This page provides a comparison of the references used in designing our luxury watch website and the resulting sections.
